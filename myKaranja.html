About Karanja Lad

Karanja Lad is located in Washim district, Maharashtra. It is well-known as the birthplace of Sant Shri Nrusimha Saraswati.

Famous Places

  • Guru Mandir
  • Gomukh Kunda
  • Jain Temples
  • Local Lakes

Culture & Food

The city celebrates Datta Jayanti and other festivals with devotion. Foods like bhakri, puran poli, and poha are enjoyed here.
